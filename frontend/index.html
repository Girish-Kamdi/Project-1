Grand Theft Auto VI is an upcoming action-adventure game in development by Rockstar Games. It is due to be the eighth main Grand Theft Auto game, following Grand Theft Auto V (2013), and the sixteenth entry overall. Set within the fictional open world state of Leonida (based on Florida) and its Miami-inspired Vice City, the story is expected to follow criminal duo Lucia and her male partner.[a] Following years of speculation and anticipation, Rockstar confirmed in February 2022 that the game was in development. That September, footage from unfinished versions was leaked online in what journalists described as one of the biggest leaks in the history of the video game industry.

Evotinction is a stealth action video game that is not yet released on Steam1. It is developed by Spikewave Games and published by Astrolabe Games. The game is set in the near future, where an AI system controlling a research facility goes rogue and starts to attack humans. The player takes the role of Dr. Liu, a researcher who infiltrates the facility to face the AI, uncover the truth, and save humanity. The game features hacking skills, service robots, and killer robots as enemies, and a story that explores the themes of evolution and extinction. The game has not received any user reviews yet, as it is still in development. However, some media outlets have covered the game and its features, such as IGN2 and Gematsu3.
